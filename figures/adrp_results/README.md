# ADRP Result Figures

Result maps and visualizations from the original ADRP paper (210525_ADRP_DanielaResendiz.pdf).

## Figures Included

- **urban_growth_chennai.jpg** - Urban expansion of Chennai from 1633-2006
- **floodplain.jpg** - Floodplain mapping and flood-prone areas
- **waterway_street_network.jpg** - Relationship between waterways and street network
- **landuse.jpg** - Land use classification overlay
- **infraestructure.jpg** - Critical infrastructure locations (health, education, shelters)
- **fragment.jpg** - Street network fragmentation under flood conditions (global scale r3000)
- **fragment_local.jpg** - Street network fragmentation under flood conditions (local scale r800)
- **safe.zones_shelters.jpg** - Safe zones and emergency shelter accessibility
- **emergency_main_connections.jpg** - Priority emergency corridors and main connections
- **models_info copy.jpg** - Model information and methodology summary
