\author{Daniela Resendiz Garcia}
\date{2021}

\begin{document}
\maketitle

\begin{abstract}
This paper introduces a spatial methodology to model accessibility loss under extreme flood conditions in Chennai, India. A 100-year return period flood scenario is overlaid on the street network to quantify where movement and access to services collapse. The analysis couples Space Syntax accessibility measures with flood exposure and supporting spatial layers to identify priority corridors and zones for safe shelter allocation and evacuation planning.
\end{abstract}
